D&D Africana Restaurant

Our Delicious Story

Welcome to D&D Afrikana Restaurant, where passion and culinary innovation come together to create a remarkable dining experience. Our journey began with a shared love for food and a vision to bring something extraordinary to the city of Winnipeg.

Founded by a family of seasoned chefs and hospitality enthusiasts, D&D Afrikana Restaurant was born out of a desire to showcase the rich tapestry of flavors from Africa while celebrating the unique local ingredients available to us. We embarked on a mission to craft a menu that marries African tradition and innovation, delivering dishes that ignite the senses and leave a lasting impression.
